On top of creating and maintaining DevStart we sometimes give workshops on the themes we cover. These workshops sometimes are part of larger events like conferences, bigger workshops, or summer schools, and sometimes we organize them on our own.

This website serves us as a small hub to provide the participants of each specific workshop with all the relevant information, materials, and resources as sometimes these are slightly different from one event to another.


Topics

Our workshops cover different topics including:

Eye-Tracking
  • Design developmentally appropriate eye-tracking experiments
  • Collect data using DeToX, our Python package for Tobii eye-trackers
  • Preprocess data with fixation classification methods optimized for young participants
  • Analyze gaze patterns and attention measures
Statistical Analysis
  • Linear and generalized mixed-effects models for complex developmental designs
  • Bayesian statistical modelling
  • Publication-ready data visualization

and we are working on expanding our themes and topics even further!
